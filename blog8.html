The Future of Music: AI or Human?

  • Alozie
  • April 2023

AI Created a Hit Song

Artificial intelligence (AI) is gaining popularity in the music industry. The recent viral success of the song "Heart On My Sleeve" created using AI to clone the voices of Drake and The Weeknd it was viewed more than 8.5 million times on TikTok and played 254,000 times on Spotify and showcase the potential of AI to transform music production, composition, audio processing, and music streaming. showcases the potential of AI to transform music production, composition, audio processing, and music streaming.

Examples of AI-powered music technology:

  • Google NSynth is a machine learning project that creates new sounds by studying the characteristics of existing sounds.
  • Flow Machines is a music composition software that uses deep learning technology to generate music compositions.
  • AIVA is an AI music composer that can create emotional soundtracks.
  • Amper Music is an AI music production software that can create license-free original music and perform.
  • Amadeus Code is an AI-powered songwriting software that can help users create instant melodies.
  • uberduck.ai is a site that can mimic the voice of pop stars.

The Future

In the future, AI could become the musician instead of an assistant to the musician. However, AI's benefits in the music industry are undeniable and can be leveraged by enthusiasts and creators to compose their own soundtracks. AI's potential to create new sounds, revolutionize music production and compose music has only just begun.
