Could ChatGPT Affect 300 million jobs ?

  • Alozie
  • April 2023

The Future of Work: Generative AI and the Threat of Automation, Design

Goldman Sachs has published a report on generative AI and its potential to disrupt the labor market. The report suggests that roughly two-thirds of current jobs are exposed to some degree of AI automation, with generative AI potentially substituting up to a quarter of current work. The legal services industry is cited as being most at risk from AI automation, but the report suggests that the new technology could also lead to significant labor cost savings and new job creation.

The report, titled "The Future of Work: Generative AI and the Threat of Automation," found that generative AI has the potential to automate a wide range of tasks, including:

  • Writing reports and other documents
  • Generating creative content, such as marketing materials and advertising
  • Translating languages
  • Performing customer service tasks
  • Making financial decisions
  • And more

The report found that the legal services industry is particularly vulnerable to AI automation, as generative AI could be used to automate tasks such as legal research, drafting contracts, and writing legal opinions. However, the report also found that AI automation could lead to new job creation in the legal services industry, such as jobs in AI development and maintenance.

Here are some of the key takeaways from the report:

  • Generative AI has the potential to automate a wide range of tasks, including writing reports, generating creative content, translating languages, performing customer service tasks, and making financial decisions.
  • The legal services industry is particularly vulnerable to AI automation, but AI automation could also lead to new job creation in the legal services industry.
  • Governments and businesses need to work together to prepare for the impact of AI automation and to ensure that everyone benefits from the new technology.

What can you do to prepare for the impact of AI automation?

There are a few things you can do to prepare for the impact of AI automation:

  • Stay up-to-date on the latest developments in AI technology.
  • Develop new skills that are in demand in the AI economy.
    • be open to learning new things.
  • Network with people who are working in AI.
  • Be prepared to change careers if necessary.

The future of work is changing rapidly, and AI is one of the key drivers of this change. By preparing for the impact of AI automation, you can ensure that you are ready for the future of work.
